Tim Berners-Lee

"He wove the World Wide Web and created a mass medium for the 21st century."

Tim Berners-Lee is best known for inventing the World Wide Web, and he has many other acclomplishments and awards.

  • 1989 - First successful communication between HTTP client and a server using the internet.

  • 2004 - Knighted by Queen Elizabeth

  • 2009 - Elected a foreign associate fo the United States National Academy of Science

  • 2011 - Became a member of the board of trustees of the Ford Foundation

  • 2017 - Received the Turing Award for inventing the World Wide Web, the first web browser, and protocols and algorithms that allow the web to scale

He wove the World Wide Web and created a mass medium for the 21st century. The World Wide Web is Berners-Lee's alone. He designed it. He loosed it on the world. And he more than anyone else has fought to keep it open, nonproprietary and free.
